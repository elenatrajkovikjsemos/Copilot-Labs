Lab 3 - Copilot Chat for Work + Web insights

Use Copilot Chat to prepare for a discussion about your company’s sales strategy and market positioning. This lab shows how to combine work grounding and web grounding for better decision-making.

Note: Work grounding uses your internal documents, emails, and organizational context. Web grounding brings in external information, industry trends, and validation.

🧩 Copilot Chat Exercise – Work + Web

Scenario

You are preparing for a discussion about your company’s sales strategy and market positioning. You will use Copilot Chat to:

  • get insights from work context
  • enrich answers with web knowledge
  • combine both for better decision-making

How to use Copilot Chat

  • Work grounding
    Use this to:
    • ask questions based on your documents, emails, and internal context
    • get contextual, organization-related insights
  • Web grounding
    Use this to:
    • bring in external information
    • compare with industry trends
    • validate or enrich internal thinking

Part 1: Work-focused prompts

Start with questions grounded in your existing content (Word/PowerPoint scenario).

  1. Prompt 1
    Ask Copilot:
    Based on this content, what are the key strengths of our sales approach? Attach the file(s) as work content, using the + button or /.
  2. Prompt 2
    Ask Copilot:
    What risks or gaps can you identify in our current sales strategy?
  3. Prompt 3
    Ask Copilot:
    How can we improve our positioning based on this material?

Part 2: Web-enhanced prompts

Now bring in external perspective.

  1. Prompt 4
    Ask Copilot:
    What are current trends in software sales for business clients?
  2. Prompt 5
    Ask Copilot:
    How do successful companies differentiate their sales strategy in this industry?
  3. Prompt 6
    Ask Copilot:
    What are common expectations from clients when choosing a software solutions provider?

Use the combined insights from work and web grounding to refine your sales strategy recommendations and prepare for your discussion.
