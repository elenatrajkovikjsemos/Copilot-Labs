🧩 Copilot in Outlook & Teams

Scenario

You are working in the sales team, handling client communication and internal collaboration.

You will use Copilot in Outlook and Teams to:

  • understand conversations quickly
  • respond professionally
  • collaborate with your team
  • summarize discussions and move work forward
⚠️ Important

This exercise works best if you have:

  • an email thread (2–4 emails)
  • a Teams chat (or create one)
  • a meeting with recording and transcript enabled

How to use Copilot

📧 In Outlook

✅ Summarize & understand

Use this to quickly understand email conversations.

✅ Draft & refine emails

Use this to create and improve replies.

💬 In Teams

✅ Rewrite messages

Use this to improve clarity and tone in chat communication.

✅ Summarize conversations

Use this to extract key points from chat or meetings.

🔹 Part 1: Outlook – Understand the conversation

Prompt 1

Summarize this email thread. Include key points, decisions, and any open questions.

Prompt 2

From this conversation, what does the client care about most?

Prompt 3

What requires the most urgent response in this thread and why?

🔹 Part 2: Outlook – Draft and refine a reply

Prompt 4

Draft a professional reply confirming our proposal, addressing the client’s concerns, and suggesting the next step.

Prompt 5

Make this email shorter and more persuasive. Add a clear call to action.

Prompt 6

Adjust the tone to be more friendly and relationship-focused, while remaining professional.

💬 Part 3: Teams – Rewrite messages (canvas experience)

Use Copilot directly in the message compose box:

  1. Type or paste a message in Teams.
  2. Select the Copilot icon in the compose area.
  3. Choose an option such as Rewrite, Make shorter, Make more formal, or Make more friendly.
  4. Review suggestions and insert the version you prefer.

💬 Part 4: Teams – Meeting and chat summary

Prompt 7

Summarize this meeting. Highlight key decisions, action items, and next steps.
⚠️ Note

Meeting summaries work best when:

  • the meeting is recorded
  • a transcript is available

This ensures Copilot can provide accurate and complete insights.

🔹 Part 5: Move the conversation forward

Prompt 10

Based on this conversation, what is the best next step to move this deal forward? Draft a short follow-up message.

End of Lab



➕ Additional capabilities in Teams meetings

Interpreter

Provides real-time translation during meetings, helping participants communicate across different languages without barriers.

Facilitator

Supports meetings by organizing discussion, tracking key points, and helping ensure that conversations stay structured and productive.
